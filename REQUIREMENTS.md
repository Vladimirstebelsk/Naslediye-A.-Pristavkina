# Requirements

This document summarizes the current teacher requirements for the school literature project website.

## Hosting and Publication

- The site can be linked from the school page under the "Projekte" section.
- The site is a static HTML/CSS website.
- The public version is hosted on Netlify.

## Required Content

The section "Разбор 'Ночевала тучка золотая'" should include the following materials.

### Персонажи

- Character schema
- Chat-style or Instagram-style material
- Banner

### Сюжет

- Time and place of action
- Timemapper link
- Collage or visualization

### Актуальность повести

- Homelessness
- Children and war
- Discussion-style or forum-like presentation

### Иллюстрации

- Separate tile or section for student illustrations

## German Visitor Support

- Add or preserve a visible translation option for German visitors.

## Review Notes

- Check the biography page for small formatting errors, especially missing spaces after punctuation.
- Do not expose private student names in public materials.
